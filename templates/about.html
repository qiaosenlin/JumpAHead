JumpAhead Executive Team

Noah Mancuso

Year: Graduate
Position: Founder, Board of Directors

More about me

Jump Ahead was created from my global health capstone project, after working in the local Chapel Hill schools. I wanted a way to combine my love of jump rope with my research interest in public health and desire to make a change in my community. Being able to turn this idea from a class assignment into a full-fledged and funded program has been an incredible learning experience, and definitely would not have been possible without the support of Carolina Jump Rope. It’s been so exciting to see our core team, and our program, continually evolve and adapt. Please don’t hesitate to reach out to us if you’d like to get involved as we grow and expand.

Graham Booth

Year: Class of 2021
Position: Founder, Board of Directors

More about me

I got involved with Jump Ahead when Noah approached me with the idea during my sophomore year. By this time, I had been jump roping for over 10 years - I thought this would be a great way to serve the local community and bring more exposure to the sport I love! Having recently begun studying nutrition, I also saw potential to include this in our approach. Working with a driven group of UNC students to bring this to life has helped me develop a refined perspective on what it means to be a public servant as well as a multitude of useful skills. I can’t wait to see how this program grows!

Morgan Parker

Year: Class of 2022
Position: Founder, Finance Team Lead

More about me

I got involved in Jump Ahead because it combines two of my interests: jump rope and public health. I had a background in jump rope before college and developed a greater interest in public health in college, so I thought Jump Ahead would be a way for me to marry these interests while getting to know the Chapel Hill community. It’s been exciting to collaborate with such a compassionate group of people and begin to assess what we can do to serve our community. I’m excited to see where Jump Ahead goes in the next few years!

Leanna Rodriguez

Year: Class of 2022
Position: Founder, Health Programs Team Lead

More about me

I joined Jump Ahead because of my personal experience with after school programs as a child. They were safe spaces for me to learn and grow, and I wanted to be a part of Jump Ahead to provide that same experience to others. I believe it’s important for children to feel accepted and cared for in their communities, and I saw Jump Ahead as a great way to create that atmosphere given children spend so much time in school. It means so much to me to be able to give back to the community with a team as passionate and driven as I am.

Carly Brockman

Year: Class of 2022
Position: Founder, HR Team Lead

More about me

I got involved with Jump Ahead through hearing Noah and Graham talking about it at Carolina Jump Rope practices during my freshman year. I have been involved in jump rope for over 10 years now, and it sounded like a great way to continue to bring jump rope to more people while helping the community. I’ve loved getting to work with this amazing group for the past couple years, and I’m super excited to see what will come in the next few years. Although I don’t have a public health background, I’ve enjoyed getting to bring in my own skills and knowledge to the team to help us continue to expand and improve over the years!

Lauren Sotell

Year: Class of 2022
Position: Research Team Lead

More about me

When I came to UNC, I was so excited to get involved by volunteering. Over the summer, I found Jump Ahead and was intrigued by their focus on using data-driven techniques to inspire action. I’m so lucky to be the newest member of the core team! Jump Ahead is an amazing organization where I can use previously collected data to better the program’s impact on exercise and nutrition, two topics for which I am deeply passionate, and research ways for our partner schools to advocate for better food choices, which I truly wish had occurred back when I was in public elementary school! Despite COVID-19 restrictions, the Zoom meetings with the team have already created an exciting atmosphere and I can’t wait to see how the organization grows and expands in the future!
