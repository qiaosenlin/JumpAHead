Jump Ahead

Making wellness accessible through interactive jump rope and nutrition activities.

What is Jump Ahead

The Jump Ahead Online Program harnesses the unique, engaging spirit of jump rope in combination with nutritious snacks to promote healthy behaviors in youth. Originally an in-person after-school program, the team created this website to deliver their snack and jump rope activities virtually.

How does it work?

Each activity is organized into a module, containing video demonstrations, written instructions, and a checklist to track progress.

Who is Jump Ahead meant for?

While we focus our activities on elementary school-aged youth, those of all ages can benefit from taking part in Jump Ahead. We strongly encourage classes, schools, or families to enroll as a group to engage in friendly competition.

Why should I join the online program?

In addition to having access to expertly-designed activities, proceeds from the online program will fund our in-person program that we offer to schools pro-bono.
